# Code of conduct

Keep discussion in issues and pull requests **on topic** and **professional**: disagree on technical merits without personal attacks.

**Not allowed:** harassment, threats, slurs, spam, or sharing someone’s private information without their permission. **Vulnerabilities** are reported through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not as “conduct” issues.

**Moderation:** maintainers may hide or remove content, or block repeat or serious abuse. Contact them via GitHub or any email they publish in the repo or their profile.

Contributions are accepted under the same terms as the project; see [LICENSE](LICENSE).
